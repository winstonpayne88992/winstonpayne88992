CONGREGATION OF DEPPER CHRISTIAN LIFE MINISTRY




Deeper Christian Life Ministry also known as Deeper Life Bible Church is a Pentecostal Christian denomination with its international headquarters, Deeper Life Bible Church Monrovia, in Liberia . It is overseen by the General Superintendent of Deeper Life Bible Church, Pastor Charles Belleh.
